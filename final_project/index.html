The Wellness Trinity

Mind

Your actions and choices create your life. The way you react to events that are out of your control also starts in your mind. Your hopes, dreams, and desire begin as thoughts that your body must enact.

Body

Your physical body is the house of your thoughts and soul. It is the physical vessel that allows you to move, eat, and do things. It is the tool and vehicle that can help or hinder you from living the life you want.

Spirit

The word spirit is derived from the Latin word "spiritus" which means breath or "spirare", to breathe. Everything that breathes has a soul.

The french believe that the soul is the immortal part of our being. It is the eternal and ephemeral aspect of our consciiousness that allows us to feel infinite, boundless, and free.
